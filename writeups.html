Team Writeups

The 18th instance of the annual programming contest series sponsored by the ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming (ICFP) ran from Friday 7 August 2015 at 12:00 UTC to Monday 10 August 2015 at 12:00 UTC.

Since the end of the contest, many teams have written about their experiences and posted their code. The following are all the team write-ups and public repositories we are aware of as of 22 September 2015 at 22:00 UTC, in alphabetical order by team name. Note that many of the write-ups are in the teams' native languages, and many repos also contain write-ups.

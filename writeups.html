Team Writeups

The 18th instance of the annual programming contest series sponsored by the ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming (ICFP) ran from Friday 7 August 2015 at 12:00 UTC to Monday 10 August 2015 at 12:00 UTC.

Since the end of the contest, many teams have written about their experiences and posted their code. The following are all the team write-ups and public repositories we are aware of as of 24 August 2015 at 17:15 UTC, in no particular order.

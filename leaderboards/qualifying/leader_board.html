Qualifying Leaderboard

This is the official qualifying leaderboard as computed from the final scoring, with the original 18 power phrases, of all submissions received during the contest.

The white leaderboard on the left has the overall standings. Each team's "score" on this leaderboard is the sum of its ranks on all the problems; lower numbers are better, with the best possible "score" being 25 (rank 1 on every problem). The black leaderboards on the right contain the individual team rankings for each of the problems. You may switch between the leaderboards by clicking on the problem names. You may highlight the entries for a particular team by clicking on the team name.
